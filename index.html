Sports For Change

What is Sports For Change?
Sports For Change is a student-driven organisation that aims to bring a change to society by bringing a fun and competitive sporting experience to everyone! Whether you are a fan of chess, basketball, or football, you will find a place to show your skills off here! Not only that, but the proceeds of all the tournaments go towards helping our children and our society!
Sports
We are offering football, paintbal, basketball, and chess in the coming months! Football took place on 14th April, 2018, and chess will be on 22nd and 23rd June 2018!
Prizes
Exciting cash prizes and vouchers for the Winners and Runners ups The cash prize for the winners of the football tournament will be Rs.7,500 and the runners' up will win Rs.5000!

Venues

The Football tournament conducted by Sports for Change, 2018 was held at Turf Park, Bangalore

The Chess tournament and the Basketball tournament conducted by Sports for Change, 2018 will be held at Oakridge International School, Bangalore

FAQ

  • Miscellaneous questions
  • What is the registration fee for the tournaments?
    The registration fee for the football event was ₹2899 per team. The registration fee for the chess tournament depends whether you have an UKCA ID or not. The initial fee is ₹500 but you have to pay ₹50 extra if you don't have the ID. The registration fee for the basketball tournament is yet to be decided.
  • How do I pay the amount?
    Before making your payment, you need to have registered on our site. Once that's done, there are two ways to make the payment - one is by PayTM and the other is by cash. If you chose the appropriate payment method during the registration, instructions on how to make the transfer should have been sent to the registered email account with all the other necessary details.
  • If I've made the payment through PayTM, do I get a confirmation?
    After you've made the payment through PayTM, one of our representatives will update that you've done so and you should receive an email regarding the completion of your payment
  • How long will the events last?
    The football event was on the 14th of April.

    The chess event is yet to be decided.

    The basketball event is yet to be confirmed
  • Who is the event being organised by?
    The event is being organised by a group of sports enthusiastic students who want to use sports to make the world a better place.
  • What will happen to the proceeds from the Event?
    All profits from events organized by Sports For Change will be donated to the NGO, Hope For Cancer Patients. More information will soon be posted on our website.
